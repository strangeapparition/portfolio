Hi! My name is Kim. I am a developer with an eye for design. My days consist mostly of developing web applications. On the side, I also design them.

Before all that I was a college student dabbling in freelance doing branding and print design, as well as web work. I love to do a bit of everything!

I have a BS in Computer Graphics Technology and a minor in Art & Design from Purdue University. My experience with digital and traditional art combined with my technical front end skills allows me to create beautiful, functional, user-centered web applications.
